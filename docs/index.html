About the Project

Pano Projection is a lightweight, high-performance Windows desktop application built with Unity. It solves the complex problem of drawing and animating on 360-degree equirectangular panoramas without distortion.

Whether you are creating concept art for VR, designing HDRi backgrounds, or animating 360-degree scenes, Pano Projection gives you a professional, non-destructive layered workflow right out of the box.

Core Features

🎨

Smart Brush Engine

Paint directly onto the panoramic sphere. Brushes scale intelligently with your FOV zoom to maintain consistent stroke width.

📚

Layer System

Organize your work with professional Paint, Animation, and Camera layers. Enjoy full opacity control and non-destructive editing.

🎬

Timeline & Animation

Animate camera movements with bezier curves and utilize Onion Skinning for precise frame-by-frame traditional animation.

📐

Dynamic Grid Snapping

Maintain perspective perfectly with customizable 3D grid overlays, 45-degree snapping, and straight-line tools.
